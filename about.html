About Collapsr

v.0.1

This app provides info on 'Collapse': The progressive decline of modern civilization.

The 'Home' page is an RSS Feed that shows general posts related to Collapse.

The 'Explore' page contains several RSS Feeds that show users related to Climate Change, Civil Unrest, Economic Inequality, Cold War 2, Prepping, Conspiracy Theories and Fictional Scenarios.

The 'Wiki' page is an easy to digest version of the /r/collapse wiki.

Collapsr pulls data from:

reddit.com/r/collapse
reddit.com/r/DieOff
reddit.com/r/StormComing
reddit.com/r/PostCollapse
